Frequently Asked Questions

What is AES-256 encryption and why should I use it?

AES-256 is a military-grade encryption standard used by governments and banks worldwide. It protects your QR code content from unauthorized access, making it perfect for sensitive information like passwords, private messages, or confidential data.

Are my QR codes stored on your servers?

No, absolutely not. All QR code generation and encryption happens entirely in your browser. No data is ever sent to our servers or stored anywhere. Your privacy and security are completely protected.

What happens if I lose my encryption key?

Encryption keys cannot be recovered if lost. This is by design for maximum security. Always save your encryption key in a secure location before downloading your encrypted QR code.

What file formats do you support for QR code images?

Our QR code scanner supports all major image formats including PNG, JPG, JPEG, WEBP, and GIF. For best results, use high-contrast images with clear QR code visibility.
