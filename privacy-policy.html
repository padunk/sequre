Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Secure QR Code Generator, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we handle data when you use our QR code generation service.

Data We DON'T Collect

Our QR code generator operates entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • The text or content you enter into QR codes
  • Generated QR code images
  • Encryption keys or passwords
  • Personal information or contact details
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Browser fingerprints or tracking data

How Our Service Works

All QR code generation and encryption processes happen locally in your web browser using JavaScript. No data is ever sent to our servers or any third-party services.

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any form of user tracking. We do not partner with advertising networks or data brokers.

Third-Party Services

We use Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) to load JavaScript libraries for QR code generation and encryption. These services may log basic access information, but they do not have access to your QR code content.

Security Measures

We implement strong security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Content Security Policy (CSP) headers
  • Subresource Integrity (SRI) for external scripts
  • No server-side data storage

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
